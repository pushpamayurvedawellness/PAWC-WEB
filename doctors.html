Founder & Lead Ayurvedic Physician

Dr. Mahathi has been drawn towards Ayurveda since the ninth grade. Not because someone pointed her there, but because the logic of it made sense to her. The idea that health is about the whole person: their constitution(prakriti), their digestion(agni), their lifestyle, not just their diagnosis. That is what drew her in.

She trained as an Ayurvedic physician (BAMS & MD(Ayu) and built her practice around classical diagnosis: Nadi Pariksha (pulse assessment), Prakriti and Vikriti evaluation, and consultations that don't rush.

A significant portion of her clinical work is in pain management: disc-related back pain, joint disorders, arthritis. Several patients have come to her after being told surgery was the only option. Some avoided it through Ayurvedic treatment.

"If you've been told nothing more can be done, or that surgery is inevitable, or that you'll have to manage your condition for life. Come in and let her look at the case. She'll give you an honest assessment, not a promise."
