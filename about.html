About Pushpam Ayurveda

The Heritage

The name Pushpam comes from a person.

Dr. Mahathi Sahukar named this clinic after her grandmother, Pushpa, not because she was a doctor, but because she defined, in the simplest way, what real care looks like.

Not hurried. Not mechanical. But patient. Attentive. Deeply present.

In her presence, people felt looked after, not through medicine, but through the quality of attention she gave. That quiet way of caring left a lasting impression.

Years later, when Dr. Mahathi built her own practice, she didn't want to create just another Ayurveda centre. She wanted to build a place where that same depth of attention becomes the foundation of healing, where patients are not just treated, but truly understood.

"So she named it Pushpam. Not as a story to tell, but as a standard to live by."

Founder's Message

A Message from Dr. Mahathi

I started learning about Ayurveda in the ninth grade. Most people decide what they want to do much later. I got lucky. It found me early.

What drew me in wasn't the theory. It was the logic. Ayurveda asks a different question: not just what is the disease, but why is this person's body out of balance? What are they eating, how are they sleeping, what does their digestion look like? The answers to those questions tell you far more than a test result.

My practice is built on one belief: food is medicine. The body, given the right conditions, knows how to heal. Most of what I do is remove the obstacles and give the body what it needs to do what it already wants to do.

Dr. Mahathi Sahukar, BAMS, MD(Ayu)

Founder & Managing Director, Pushpam Ayurveda Wellness Centre

Our Method

Our Philosophy of Care

Ayurveda doesn't treat a disease. It treats the person who has the disease.

We Assess

We analyze your baseline constitution (Prakriti), current imbalances (Vikriti), digestion strength, and stress routines over detailed consultations.

We Design

We build a personal roadmap consisting of classical formulations, supervised clinical therapies, and diet protocols matching your recovery timeline.

We Guide

We support you with habit modifications and progressive check-ups to lock in your metabolic recovery and safeguard against future flares.

What Patients Consistently Say

Dr. Mahathi explains the logic behind every step. Patients leave knowing why they are taking a medicine, what a therapy is doing, and what to expect. That transparency is what makes people follow through.

She takes her time

Consultations aren't 10 minutes. If you've felt rushed through a medical appointment before and left with more questions than answers, this will feel different.

Classical therapies

Panchakarma at spas is a wellness service. At Pushpam, it's a clinical intervention: prescribed by a doctor, supervised throughout. There's a difference.

She won't tell you Ayurveda cures everything. She'll give you an honest assessment and realistic expectations. Patients appreciate that.
