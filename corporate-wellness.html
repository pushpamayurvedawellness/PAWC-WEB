Workplace Well-being

Supporting Employee Wellbeing Through Preventive Health

Chronic fatigue, back pain, poor digestion, disrupted sleep, unmanaged stress. These don't announce themselves. They build quietly until they show up as reduced focus, more sick days, or burnout.

Most of them are preventable. And far easier to address early than to treat later.

Pushpam Ayurveda works with organizations in Bangalore to introduce practical, evidence-informed preventive health initiatives into the workplace. Sessions are designed for non-medical audiences, built around the realities of modern work life.

Wellness Suites

Programs Offered

Lifestyle Disorder Awareness Sessions

60 to 90 mins

Interactive sessions introducing employees to common lifestyle-related health concerns and practical Ayurvedic habits they can start using immediately.

  • How work routines, irregular meals, and stress create physical imbalance
  • Understanding your body type and what it means for energy and digestion
  • Simple daily habits from Ayurveda that take under 10 minutes
  • The gut-brain axis, particularly relevant for knowledge workers
Ideal format: Lunch-and-learn or wellness day addition

Stress Management Workshops

Half Day

Explores the physiology of chronic stress and builds practical resilience. Not about ignoring pressure. It is about building the capacity to handle it without depleting the nervous system.

  • Breathing techniques (Pranayama) that regulate the nervous system immediately
  • Dietary and lifestyle adjustments that build resilience over time
  • How to identify your stress type and respond appropriately
Focus: Practical autonomic nervous system cooling
