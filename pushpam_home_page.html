Our Roots

Named for her grandmother. Built on a lifetime of Ayurveda.

Read more about Dr. Mahathi and Pushpam →

Dr. Mahathi Sahukar has been drawn to Ayurveda since highschool. She named this clinic Pushpam in honour of her grandmother Pushpa, the person who showed her what it means to care for someone's health with patience and attention.

That spirit shapes how she practises today. Consultations aren't rushed. She takes time to understand what's actually going on: your digestion, your sleep, your stress. She explains it back to you, so you understand too.

Her core belief: food is medicine. The body knows how to heal. It usually just needs the right conditions.
