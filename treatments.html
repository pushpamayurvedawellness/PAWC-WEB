Spine, Joint & Back Pain

Effective pain management and L4-L5-S1 disc restoration protocols to avoid surgery and restore mobility.

One of the most common reasons patients come to Pushpam, often after they've already tried other options. We've seen patients with L4-L5-S1 disc bulges and L5-S1 slip discs find significant relief through Ayurvedic treatment, including several who avoided surgery.

Treatment typically includes Kati Basti (localized oil therapy for the lower back), pottali swedas (herbal poultice), Abhyanga, and internal medicines. Dr. Mahathi will review your reports and give you an honest assessment of what is realistically possible for your case.

Digestive Disorders

Rebuilding digestive fire (Agni) to clear systemic toxins (Ama) and restore metabolic harmony.

In Ayurveda, digestion is the foundation of everything. When Agni (the digestive fire) is compromised, it creates downstream problems: low energy, skin issues, weight gain, brain fog. Many chronic conditions that don't seem digestive actually start here.

We treat chronic acidity, bloating, IBS-type symptoms, and sluggish metabolism. Treatment involves clearing accumulated toxins (Ama) and rebuilding digestive strength through Panchakarma, herbal medicines, and dietary guidance.

Chronic Skin Conditions

Purifying internal blood tissues (Rakta) and cooling Pitta heat to resolve acne, eczema, and psoriasis.

Skin problems in Ayurveda are rarely just skin problems. Chronic acne, eczema, and psoriasis are typically expressions of internal imbalance, usually involving the blood (Rakta Dhatu) and Pitta aggravation, compounded by poor digestion.

Apoorva came to Pushpam after consulting three allopathy hospitals with no results. Within 15 days of Panchakarma, she noticed visible improvement. Treatment is always individual. Two patients with psoriasis will not receive the same protocol.

PCOD & Women's Health

Targeting digestive imbalances, managing stress levels, and regulating menstrual cycles naturally.

PCOD and hormonal disorders are traced in Ayurveda to their root: usually a combination of digestive imbalance, chronic stress, and disrupted sleep. Treatment at Pushpam may include specific formulations, targeted Panchakarma, and lifestyle guidance.

Dr. Mahathi works with irregular menstruation, PCOD, hormonal imbalance, and related fatigue and weight concerns.

Thyroid Management

Addressing underlying thyroid dysfunctions by treating metabolic processes and Agni health.

Ayurveda approaches thyroid dysfunction by addressing the underlying metabolic and digestive imbalances that affect thyroid function, not just managing hormone levels. Archana came in with a thyroid condition and noticed clear improvement through Ayurvedic treatment.

Stress, Burnout & Insomnia

Rebuilding vital energy reserves (Ojas) and settling Vata hyper-reactivity in the nervous system.

Chronic stress in Ayurveda is understood as a depletion of Ojas (vital energy), caused by overwork, irregular routines, and inadequate sleep. Left unaddressed, it becomes fatigue, anxiety, insomnia, and eventually physical illness.

Pushpam offers calming therapies including Shirodhara, herbal nervous system support, and structured lifestyle guidance. The goal is to rebuild Ojas, not just manage symptoms.

Preventive Care & Detox

Proactive health consultations, seasonal Panchakarma cleanses, and custom lifestyle modifications.

You don't have to be unwell to come to Pushpam. Seasonal Panchakarma, wellness consultations, and diet & lifestyle counselling are available for patients who want to maintain their health rather than react when something goes wrong.

Clinical Interventions

Therapies We Use

All therapies are prescribed by Dr. Mahathi based on your diagnosis. Nothing is offered as a menu.

Panchakarma

The classical Ayurvedic detox system: preparatory treatments followed by procedures such as Virechana, Vamana, Basti, and Nasya. Duration: typically 7 to 21 days, decided after consultation.

Kati Basti

Localized oil retention therapy for the lower back. A warm medicated oil pool is held at the lumbar region. Particularly effective for disc-related pain.

Kizhi/Pottali Sweda

Herbal poultice therapy. Heated bundles applied rhythmically to reduce inflammation, improve circulation, and relieve joint and muscular pain.

Abhyanga

Full-body therapeutic oil massage with medicated oils. Supports circulation and nervous system function. Often used to prepare the body before Panchakarma.

Shirodhara

A continuous warm oil stream poured over the forehead. Deeply calming. Used for stress, anxiety, sleep disorders, and neurological conditions.

Medicines

Classical Ayurvedic formulations (kashayas, tablets, capsules, churnas, etc.) prescribed individually based on your constitution(prakriti) and condition.
